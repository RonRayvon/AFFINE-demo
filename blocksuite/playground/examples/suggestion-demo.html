BlockSuite Suggestion Demo

This demo shows how to create, apply, and reject suggestions in BlockSuite.

Suggestion

Instructions:
  • Click on any block to select it
  • Use the buttons above to create different types of suggestions
  • Old blocks (to be replaced/deleted) will have a gray background
  • New blocks (to be inserted/replacing) will have a light green background
  • Click on a block with a suggestion to see Apply/Reject options
  • Apply and Reject operations support undo/redo
